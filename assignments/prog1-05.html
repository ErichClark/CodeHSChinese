1.05 Short Stack 少量堆疊

Objective 目標:

Write a program to have Karel create a short stack of tennis balls. Karel should move one spot, put down two tennis balls, and then move again.
寫一個程式讓卡羅爾創造一個網球堆。卡羅爾應移動至另一個點，放下兩個網球，然後再次移動。

Ending World 成果圖示

Karel's Commands 卡羅爾的指令

  • move(); 移動();
  • turnLeft(); 左轉();
  • putBall(); 放球();
  • takeBall(); 拿球();
