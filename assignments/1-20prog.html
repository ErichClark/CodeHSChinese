Program 1.20 Pancakes 煎餅

Objective 目標:

Karel is the waiter. He needs to deliver a stack of pancakes to the guests on the 2nd, 4th, and 6th avenue. Each stack of pancakes should have three pancakes.
卡雷爾是服務員。他在送一疊煎餅給客人2號，第4和第6大道。煎餅的每個堆棧應該有三個煎餅。
Create a function called makePancakes() to help karel solve this problem. The world should end up exactly as shown here.
創建一個名為做煎餅（）的函數來幫助卡雷爾解決這個問題。世界應該結束了完全相同，如下所示。

Starting World and Ending World 世界開始和結束的世界

  • "Defining a function" is teaching karel *how* to do it. 定義功能”教學卡雷爾**怎麼做
  • "Calling a function" is saying "hey karel, do it!" “調用函數”的說“哎卡雷爾，做到這一點！”
  • You only "define" a function once. Then you can "call" it many times. 只有你“定義”功能一次。然後你可以“呼叫”了很多次
