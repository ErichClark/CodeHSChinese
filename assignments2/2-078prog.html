2.78 Assignment: Fibonacci 分配：斐波那契

Objective 目標:

Write a program that prints out the numbers in the Fibonacci sequence up until the max number. You can figure out the next number in the Fibonacci sequence by adding the two previous numbers. The first number is 1 and the second number is 1. To get the third number we take 1 + 1 = 2. To get the fourth number we take the second number plus the third number (1 + 2 = 3). Here is a sample run of the program.
寫，打印出來的數字，在斐波那契序列，直到最大數量的程序。您可以通過將前兩個數字計算出的斐波那契序列中的下 一個數字。第一個數字是1，第二個數字是1。要得到我們取1+1 =2，要得到我們需要的第二數量加上第三個數字（1 +2= 3） 的第四號，第三號。下面是該程序的運行示例。
