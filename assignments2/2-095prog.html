2.95 Assignment: Horizontal Lines 分配：水平线

Objective 目標:

Write a function that draws horizontal lines on the graphics canvas. If a line is horizontal, then the y-values for the endpoints are the same. The parameters to your function should be the y location, and the length, and all of your lines should start at x position 0.
