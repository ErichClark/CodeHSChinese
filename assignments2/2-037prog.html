2.37 Assignment: Can You Graduate? 任务：你可以毕业？

Objective 目標:

Ask the user for two boolean values. The first should be stored in a variable telling whether or not they have enough units (credits), and the second should be stored in a variable telling whether or not they have met the requirements. They can only graduate if they have enough units, AND have met the requirements. Print out whether they can graduate.
問兩個布爾值的用戶。首先應該被存儲在一個變量算命不管他們是否有足夠的單位（學分），第二個應該被存儲在一個變量算命無論他們是否已符合要求。 他們只能畢業，如果他們有足夠的單位，並已符合要求。打印出他們是否能畢業。
