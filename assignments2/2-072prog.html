2.72 Assignment: Lots of Dice 分配：很多骰子

Objective 目標:

Write a program that rolls a 6-sided die 100 times, and prints out all of the rolls. (If you are feeling adventurous, can you count the number of 6s that you rolled?)
編寫一個程序，推出了6面的骰子100次，並打印出所有的軋輥。（如果你喜歡冒險，你能指望的次數，你卷了6？）
