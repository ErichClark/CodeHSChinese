2.56 Assignment: Chalk Board 分配：粉笔板

Objective 目標:

You have just gotten in trouble in school for coming late. The punishment is that you have to write "I will not come late to school" 100 times on the board. That would take you a while to do... but luckily you have for loops. Using a for loop, print "I will not come late to school" 100 times.
你剛剛得到在學校的麻煩來晚了。懲罰是，你必須寫“我不會遲到學校”100次在黑板上。這將需要一段時間做的......但幸運的是你有一個循環。 使用為循環，打印“我不會遲到學校”100次。
