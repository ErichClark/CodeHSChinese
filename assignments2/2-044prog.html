2.44 Assignment: All Star 作业：全明星

Objective 目標:

Write a program that gets three variables from the user, their points per game, their rebounds per game, and their assists per game. We need to figure out if the user is an all star. They are an all star if they got at least 25 points per game, OR they got at least 10 points, at least 10 rebounds, and at least 10 assists. You will need to use both logical and comparison operators here.
編寫一個程序，從用戶獲取三個變量，每場比賽他們的積分，每場比賽他們的籃板，每場比賽的助攻。我們需要弄清楚，如果用戶是一個全明星。他們是一個全明星， 如果他們得到了每場比賽至少得到25分，或者他們得到至少10分，至少10個籃板，和至少10次助攻。你將需要使用邏輯和比較操作符在這裡。
